Monkey D. Luffy

About Luffy

Monkey D Luffy is a guy who ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi - The Gum-Gum Fruit and gained super powers. He travels the Seas with his crew of friends to try to find the One Piece and become the next Pirate King.
